Garlic Pasta

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients:

200g spaghetti, 3 cloves garlic, minced, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, Chopped parsley for garnish, Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until golden brown.

3. Toss cooked spaghetti in the garlic-infused oil.

4. Add grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
