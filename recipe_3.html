Penne Pasta

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients:

250g penne pasta, 1/2 cup tomato sauce, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, 2 cloves garlic, minced, Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Cook penne pasta according to package instructions.

2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until fragrant.

3. Add tomato sauce and cook until heated through.

4. Toss cooked penne pasta in the sauce, then add grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
