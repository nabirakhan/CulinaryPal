Vegetarian Stir-Fry

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients:

1 cup broccoli florets, 1 cup sliced bell peppers, 1 cup sliced carrots, 1 cup snap peas, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 3 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp honey

Instructions:

1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.

2. Sauté garlic until fragrant, then add broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas.

3. Stir-fry the vegetables until they are crisp-tender.

4. Add soy sauce and honey, tossing to coat the vegetables evenly.

5. Continue cooking until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables.
