Biryani

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice, 500g chicken, cut into pieces, 1 large onion, thinly sliced, 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 2 tbsp biryani masala, 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves, 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves, Ghee for cooking

Instructions:

1. Rinse basmati rice and soak in water for 30 minutes. Drain.

2. Sauté sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for later use.

3. Add chicken to the pan and cook until browned. Add yogurt, biryani masala, mint, and coriander leaves.

4. Cook rice in a separate pot until 70% done. Layer half the rice over the chicken, sprinkle with ghee, and top with reserved onions.

5. Finish cooking on low heat until rice is fully cooked. Serve hot.
